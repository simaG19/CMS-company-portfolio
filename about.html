About Us

We waive our fees to consult start-up innovators in health.


Despite being one of the oldest countries in history with an ever-expanding economy, Ethiopia’s healthcare system doesn’t seem to be keeping up. It yearns for those in healthcare with the opportunity to examine the system closely, to look for feasible answers to the problems holding it back. We were thus compelled to explore all means to assist in this endeavour after realizing the depth of potential that exists. With a vision of just what could be accomplished through cooperation and innovation, Sustainable Healthcare Solutions was born.

We at SHS are concerned with bringing change that is lasting and internal. We aim to use the resources, expertise, and knowledge available to us to help establish a sustainable healthcare system in Ethiopia. We hope to do this by developing meaningful and robust partnerships with like-minded organizations and working to realize our collective visions.

Our team of specialists is prepared to dedicate their time, knowledge, and passion to finding long-term solutions. They have extensive backgrounds in healthcare management, health infrastructure construction, consulting, and ICT. Our team is richly experienced in critical processes including but not limited to supply chain management, leadership, quality healthcare delivery, project management, hospital development, and capacity building. Our goal is to offer comprehensive support for efficient healthcare delivery in Ethiopia.
