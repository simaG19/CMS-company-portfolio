Turn-key Project Management

Fundamentally, ‘turn-key’ describes a form of engagement between a client and an implementing firm in which the firm takes on the responsibility of developing a product that is complete and ready to use right away. A turn-key project is one that is conceived, constructed, and equipped with all necessary facilities by an implementing firm and handed over to the client complete and ready for operation. We at SHS, have developed a business-friendly and cost-effective model to implement this form of project management in the health programs’ arena. We take the concept of a project from its inception- plan it, execute it, monitor and control the process and seamlessly integrate it into the client’s operations in a customized manner. This significantly reduces the hassles involved with planning and executing projects from the side of the client. Give us your project and see it grow expeditiously. We work strictly on your behalf.
